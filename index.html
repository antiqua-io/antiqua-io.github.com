Welcome to Antiqua's Grave

Antiqua was a way to archive private GitHub repositories so that you could free up space and save a little bit on your monthly bill. I'm of the opinion that billing for private repository slots is a poor choice and this was my response. Since it wasn't a successful project in terms of user adoption, I'm open sourcing it under the MIT license.
